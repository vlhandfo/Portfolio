Correlation:

Correlation is expressed as number in the interval [-1, 1] and is not the probability that something occurs.

Positive - there is a positive correlation between the two features: as one goes up, the other goes up.
Zero - there is no correlation, positive or negative between the two features.
Negative - there is a negative correlation between the two features: as one goes up, the other goes down and vice versa.

Covariance vs. Correlation

covariance: the direction of the linear relationship between variables
correlation: the strength and direction of the linear relationship

Conclusions:

  • Women had the best chance of surviving, and children had a pretty good chance, but men did not.
    • Less than 1 in 5 men on the Titanic, (\(< 20 \%\)), survived
    • Almost 4 out of 5 women, (\(\approx 80 \%\)), survived.
